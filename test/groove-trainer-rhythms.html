How it works

The bar fills with the note value you pick. Hit Play — you get a one-bar count-in, then the bar loops. Read the notation and play each note to the click.

Center is whether your average lands on your chosen feel (on / ahead / behind). Evenness is how repeatable you are, regardless of center. Rhythm is whether you played the right notes — missed notes and extra notes pull it down. Calibrate first (play steady on the beat ~15 hits) so Center reads true; Evenness is latency-proof either way.
