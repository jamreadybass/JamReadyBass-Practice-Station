How to use PocketLocker

1 · Start audio. On the Play tab, tap Enable mic & click and allow the microphone. Headphones are best. PocketLocker calibrates to your room automatically.

2 · Calibrate timing. When prompted, play steady quarter notes on the click (~15 hits) so your scores read true. Re-calibrate if you switch headphones.

3 · Pick a level. On the Levels tab, use The Challenge to work through levels in order, or switch to Freeplay to build your own rhythm.

4 · Play to the pocket. Notes roll along the highway. Play each note the instant its gem center crosses the amber pocket line.

5 · Read your scores. Center = on your chosen feel. Evenness = consistency. Rhythm = right notes. Clearing a Challenge level earns up to three stars and unlocks the next.

Troubleshooting

Notes aren't registering. Open Sound & timing setup and watch the level meter as you play. Raise Sensitivity until your notes clear the amber line.

Phantom / extra notes. Tap Measure my room (stay silent). Keep Speaker mode on. Headphones help most.

Gems don't line up (Bluetooth lag). Adjust Highway sync with the −5 / +5 buttons until a gem meets the click. Never affects your score.

Phone, no headphones. Turn on Visual-only beat — the click goes silent and the pocket pulses; play to the pulse.

Score feels unfair. Make sure you calibrated this session; re-calibrate if you changed headphones.
